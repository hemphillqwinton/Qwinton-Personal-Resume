Who am I?

I am a motivated engineering and aviation student at Elizabeth City State University, pursuing dual bachelor's degrees in Engineering Technology and Aviation Science. I am driven by a passion for both technical excellence and continuous personal development. I take pride in being disciplined, dependable, and committed to high standards in everything I do.

My Ambitions

Professionally, I have hands-on experience in aircraft maintenance and repair through my work with Appalachian Aero Group, where I assisted in inspections, diagnostics, and maintenance on aircraft ranging from small trainers to business jets. I am comfortable working in high-responsibility environments that demand precision, safety awareness, and teamwork. Studying both engineering technology and aviation science is allowing me to become an engineer but also have a solid foundation in aviation which is why I am pursuing my Private Pilot License

Alongside my technical background, I bring leadership experience as the founder and president of my university's tennis club and as a class council officer, which has strengthened my organizational and communication skills. I am passionate about aviation, engineering, and problem-solving, and I am constantly seeking opportunities to grow, contribute, and make a meaningful impact. I value integrity, teamwork, and discipline, and I aim to build a career that combines technical skill, leadership, and service.
